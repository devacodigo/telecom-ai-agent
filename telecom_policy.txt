DEUTSCHE TELEKOM CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY

1. REFUND POLICY
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
To request a refund, customers must contact support with their order ID.
Partial refunds are available for unused months on annual plans.

2. DATA PLANS
Basic Plan: 10GB data per month at 4G speeds. Price: €15/month.
Standard Plan: 50GB data per month at 4G speeds. Price: €25/month.
Premium Plan: Unlimited data at 5G speeds. Price: €45/month.
Data rollover is available on Standard and Premium plans only.

3. NETWORK OUTAGES
In case of network outage exceeding 24 hours, customers receive automatic bill credit.
Credit amount equals 1 day of their monthly plan cost.
Customers can report outages via the app, website, or by calling 0800-123-4567.

4. CONTRACT TERMS
Minimum contract period is 12 months.
Early termination fee is €50 or remaining months value, whichever is lower.
Contract renewal offers are sent 60 days before expiry.

5. INTERNATIONAL ROAMING
Roaming is available in 50+ countries.
Standard roaming rate: €0.05/MB data, €0.15/minute calls.
International roaming pass: €10/day for unlimited calls and 1GB data.
Roaming must be activated 24 hours before travel.

6. CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Support is available 24/7 via phone, chat, and email.
Premium customers receive priority support with response time under 1 hour.
Standard customers receive response within 24 hours.
Support phone number: 0800-123-4567
Support email: support@telekom.de
